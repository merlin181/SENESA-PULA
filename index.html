SENESA-PULA GROUP

Senesa-Pula Group is a proudly Basotho-owned company dedicated to empowering FMCG brands through exceptional sales execution, in-store merchandising, and cross-border trade support. We manage the full journey of products—from border entry to store shelves—ensuring seamless market entry and reliable distribution. Inspired by our name, which means “the rainmaker,” we strive to create growth, opportunity, and sustainable success for every brand we represent.
