About Senesa-Pula Group

Senesa-Pula Group is a fully Basotho-owned company that collaborates with FMCG brands to deliver sales execution and in-store merchandising services. Additionally, we provide cross-border sales agent support at crucial trade locations. We oversee the entire process of transporting products from the border to the local market, ensuring a seamless transition from importation to shelf placement.

The name “Senesa-Pula” translates to “the rainmaker.” Our guiding principle is simple: we strive to bring growth, progress, and opportunity to every brand we represent.
